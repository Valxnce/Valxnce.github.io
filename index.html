About Vortex Research Lab

Vortex Research Lab conducts advanced experimental and theoretical physics research with a focus on particle instrumentation, precision metrology, and quantum technologies. We collaborate internationally and publish open datasets and software on GitHub.

Particle Detection

Design and testing of next-generation detectors for high-energy physics experiments and cosmic particle observatories.

Quantum Sensors

Development of atomic and solid-state sensors for precision measurements and gravitational tests.

Theoretical Models

Modeling of singularities, quantum gravity approaches, and simulations for early-universe conditions.
