Exploring the Frontiers of Physics

About Vortex Lab

Vortex Lab is a state-of-the-art research center dedicated to unraveling the mysteries of the universe. Led by Head Physicist Nimish Upadhyay, we explore quantum mechanics, particle physics, and astrophysical phenomena. Our mission is to push the boundaries of human understanding through innovation and collaboration.

Research Areas

Quantum Mechanics

Investigating entanglement, superposition, and quantum field theory for breakthroughs in computation and physics.

Particle Physics

Exploring fundamental particles using high-energy accelerators and detectors inspired by CERN technologies.

Astrophysics

Studying black holes, wormholes, and cosmic singularities to understand the deepest secrets of space-time.

Ongoing Projects

Quantum Wormhole Simulation

Developing advanced models for traversable wormholes using cutting-edge quantum theory.

Dark Matter Detection

Designing ultra-sensitive detectors to reveal the elusive nature of dark matter particles.

Singularity Problem

Exploring new theories that could solve the black hole singularity paradox.
